About Me

I am Josh.

A lover of boardgames, art and anything geeky in-between.

I started out as an artist, I focused on both the business and creative side of the art world. My biggest passion was the digital arts and creating stories from a single image.

I then moved onto management in the hospitality industry, where not only was I loved by my team, I was voted general manager of the year for Wales and West.

However, I had always wanted to delve into the world of programming, and after two years of self-teaching, I enrolled in iO academy to study as a full stack developer and expand my knowledge.

Through coding, I have found a way to be creative, solve problems and to tell a story.
